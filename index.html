Dr.Chiranjevi Venkatesh

Born in Vijayawada,Krishna District,Andhra Pradesh. His journey into medical field began from Siddhardha medical college where he pursued his MBBS and went on to specialize in Cardiology. An alumnus of the illustrious AIIMS institute, New Delhi, Dr. Chiranjevi Venkatesh did his M.D, D.M Cardiology under Prof. M.L.Bhatia with a goal to provide the best possible medical care to people from all sections of the society. To achieve this he chose to serve the people of Vijayawada and surrounding rural areas in spite of lucrative opportunities abroad.Medical expertise to all sections of society, technology within reach of common man and preventive care through building awareness have been the areas of primary focus for Dr. Chiranjevi Venkatesh. He has to his credit many initiatives and awareness walks in lieu of bringing into public the importance of staying healthy through lifestyle changes and medical awareness.
